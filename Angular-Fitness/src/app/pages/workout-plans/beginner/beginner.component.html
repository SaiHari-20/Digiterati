Beginner Workout Plan

Chest

  • Push-Ups: 3x10-15
  • Chest Press Machine: 3x8-12
  • Incline Machine Press: 3x8-12
  • Pec Deck Machine: 3x10-12
  • Dumbbell Floor Press: 3x8-12

Back

  • Lat Pulldown: 3x8-12
  • Seated Row: 3x8-12
  • Superman Hold: 3x15-20s
  • Dumbbell Bent-Over Rows: 3x10-12
  • Resistance Band Pull Aparts: 3x12-15

Shoulders

  • Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3x8-12
  • Lateral Raises: 3x10-12
  • Reverse Fly: 3x10-12
  • Front Raises: 3x10-12
  • Shoulder Press Machine: 3x8-12

Arms

  • Bicep Curls: 3x10-12
  • Tricep Dips (Bench): 3x10-12
  • Hammer Curls: 3x10-12
  • Cable Tricep Pushdowns: 3x8-12
  • EZ Bar Curls: 3x10-12

Legs

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3x10-15
  • Leg Press Machine: 3x10-12
  • Seated Leg Curl: 3x10-12
  • Calf Raises: 3x12-15
  • Dumbbell Lunges: 3x8-12 per leg
